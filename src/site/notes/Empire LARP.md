## Info
- Largest LARP in Europe, over 4000 people camp over the weekend
    - More than a Roman legion!
- Empire has been running since Easter 2013, over 50 events
- Run by [Profound Decisions](https://www.profounddecisions.co.uk/aboutus?13), a professional group running LARPs since 2004
    - I was very impressed with how organised they are, especially their [conduct](https://www.profounddecisions.co.uk/empire-wiki/Conduct) rules and [children](https://www.profounddecisions.co.uk/empire-wiki/Children) policies
- Location: [Steeplechase LRP Centre](https://www.profounddecisions.co.uk/empire-wiki/Steeplechase_LRP_Centre), Beach Road CB24 8FP
    - 20 mins taxi from Cambridge Station
    - (The land was recently purchased by a syndicate of players!)
- Price: £90 fri-sat, pay on the gate.
    - £10 surcharge if arriving thursday, that's good value
- Facilities
    - Showers!
    - Toilets!
    - Food vendors!
    - Mains water taps available all over the site for drinking and cleaning
